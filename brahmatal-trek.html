Overview

The Brahmatal Trek, set in the picturesque landscapes of Uttarakhand, India, offers a breathtaking experience for adventure enthusiasts. Known for its stunning views and challenging trails, this trek takes you through serene forests, high-altitude campsites, and magnificent summits.

The journey leads to the beautiful Brahmatal, offering an exhilarating trekking experience with picturesque landscapes and a chance to explore the natural beauty of the Himalayas.

05 Nights/06 Days

Itinerary

Day 01: KATHGODAM/DEHRADUN – LOHAJUNG (220kms/09-10 Hrs) Height: 2316MTS


    • Morning the group will assemble at the pickup point at 06:00 AM. Then drive towards Lohajung village which is the base camp for the Brahmatal Trek. On arrival at Lohajung, check in to the designated Hotel. The rest of the day is free for leisure and travelers are advised to rest. In the evening, Dinner and overnight stay in Lohajung.

      • Arrive at Pickup point at 06:00 AM

      Drive towards Lohajung

      Stay in Lohajung Village

Day 02: LOHAJUNG – BEKALTAL (6kms Trek/4-5Hrs) Height: 3012 MTS


    • Early morning after breakfast, a small briefing session would be conducted by the trek leader where all the necessary details about the rest of the day will be shared. Then we will start our trekking journey. En route to your campsite, you would be traveling through lush green forests consisting of pine trees with breathtaking mountain views. You will reach Bekaltal after five hours of trekking. After reaching your campsite, you will have lunch. The rest of the day is at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at Bekaltal Campsite.

      • Listen to all the instructions of trek leader carefully

      Check out from the Hotel

      Start your trek for Bekaltal

      Stay at Bekaltal

Day 03: BEKALTAL – BRAHMATAL (7kms Trek/5-6Hrs) Height: 3182 MTS


    • Morning after having tea and breakfast, your Trek Leader will give you a briefing about the day before we proceed. After that, you will start the trek towards the Brahmatal Base Camp, which takes about 5-6 hours. On arrival, check in to the campsite, after freshening up have a warm lunch. The rest of the day is at leisure and you can relax by indulging in activities like snowball fights and other fun games. Dinner and overnight stay at Brahmatal Base Camp.

      • Check out from the Bekaltal

      Start your trek for Brahmatal

      Stay at Brahmatal Base Camp

Day 04: BRAHMATAL – DALDUM CAMP (6kms Trek/6-7 Hrs) Height: 3774MTS


    • Morning after breakfast, you will finally begin your trek towards Brahmatal Summit. After reaching the summit, you can enjoy the view from the top and witness the beautiful sunrise. Shortly afterward, trek down to Daldum Camp. After reaching, Lunch will be served at the campsite.

      • Start your trek for Brahmatal Summit

      Trek down to Daldum Camp

      Stay at Daldum Camp

Day 05: DALDUM CAMP – LOHAJUNG (3kms Trek/3-4 Hrs) Height: 2316MTS


    • Morning after breakfast, trek down to Lohajung. After reaching, Lunch will be served at the hotel. It’s the last night at the campsite, so you will have a farewell bash. Dinner and Overnight stay at Lohajung.

      • Trek down to Lohajung

      Stay at Lohajung

Day 06: KATHGODAM/DEHRADUN – LOHAJUNG (220kms/09-10 Hrs) Height: 2316MTS


    • Morning after breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed back to Dehradun/Kathgodam. The group will disassemble here and be on their separate ways with a heart full of contentment and a mind full of memories.

      • Check out from the Hotel

      Drop at Dehradun / Kathgodam
