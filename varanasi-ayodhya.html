Overview

Discover the spiritual essence of India with a 3 Nights/4 Days tour through Varanasi and Ayodhya. Experience the sacred Ganga Aarti, visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and take a serene boat ride on the Ganges in Varanasi. In Ayodhya, explore the Ram Janmabhoomi and Hanuman Garhi Temple. This tour offers a deep dive into the rich religious history and vibrant culture of two of India’s holiest cities.

03 Nights/04 Days

Itinerary

Day 01: ON ARRIVAL VARANASI

  • On arrival in Varanasi, proceed to the hotel. After checking in and freshening up, enjoy some local sightseeing. Then return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.
Day 02: VARANASI – AYODHYA (215 kms/4-5 Hrs)
  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple and Ganga Ghat. Later, proceed to Ayodhya. On arrival, check in at the hotel, followed by dinner and an overnight stay.
Day 03: FULL DAY AT AYODHYA
  • After breakfast, visit the Ram Mandir, Hanumangarhi, and other local sites in Ayodhya. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.
Day 04: DEPARTURE
  • Morning after breakfast, check out from the hotel and then proceed to Ayodhya station.
