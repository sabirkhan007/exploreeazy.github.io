03 Nights/04 Days
DEHRADUN – KEDARNATH – BADRINATH
Rs.125,000/- PerPerson(Including 5%GST)

Itinerary

Day 01: SAHASTRADHARA HELIPAD – SERSI – KEDARNATH


    • On arrival at Sahastradhara Helipad, we fly to Sersi Helipad, followed by a flight to Kedarnath from the same helipad.
    • The Kedarnath temple is approximately 500 meters from the helipad, taking about 15 minutes to reach.
    • A Pandit will lead the group for VIP darshan at Kedarnath Temple.
    • After darshan and pooja, the group will fly back to Sersi Helipad and stay overnight at Guptkashi near the Mandakini river.

      • Sahastradhara Helipad to Sersi Helipad

      Sersi to Kedarnath and VIP Darshan

      Overnight stay at Guptkashi

Day 02: SERSI – BADRINATH (MANA VILLAGE TOUR)


    • The group will depart from Sersi Helipad to Badrinath Helipad.
    • Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and visit Mana village, the last Indian border village near China, for sightseeing.
    • In the evening, attend Swarna Aarti at Badrinath temple, followed by dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel.

      • Flight from Sersi to Badrinath Helipad

      Sightseeing at Mana Village

      Evening Swarna Aarti at Badrinath Temple

      Dinner and overnight stay at Badrinath

Day 03: BADRINATH – DEHRADUN (DEPARTURE)


    • After breakfast, the group will fly back to Dehradun. En route, enjoy stunning aerial views of confluences including Vishnu Prayag, Nanda Prayag, Karna Prayag, Rudra Prayag, and Dev Prayag.
    • The group will be dropped at the helipad, marking the end of the tour with cherished memories.

      • Flight back to Dehradun

      Aerial view of the confluences

      End of tour at Dehradun helipad
